Welcome to the ‘TEENS CAN CODE’ Community

Teens Can Code

Teens Can Code is a not-for-profit organization helping teenagers living in low-income communities of Nigeria access coding classes, by bringing computer programming courses directly to schools, we teach students ages 9-20years how to code, through our immersive free code classes, workshops, events, and after school programs. We also help our students secure remote jobs, summer jobs and internship opportunities.

We are a group of smart, dedicated professionals who share a passion for inspiring young people to realize how fun and rewarding computer science can be, and how it can open doors to amazing careers and even solve big societal problems.
